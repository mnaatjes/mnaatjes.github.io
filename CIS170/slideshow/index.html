Rooters at Pittsburgh Ball Grounds, 1903 World Series Description:

During game four of the World Series between the Boston Americans and Pittsburgh Pirates. Pittsburgh is at bat.

Although the Americans lost the game to fall behind three games to one, it was considered the turning point of the series with the Rooters spurring Boston to win in eight games.
